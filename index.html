When you're on a maintenance treatment regimen that includes an oral medication and infusion, such as LYNPARZA + bevacizumab, it helps to be organized and plan ahead. Here are a few ways to prepare for your appointments and stay on track.

TIPS TO GET THE MOST OUT OF TREATMENT
  • Ask Questions – Your doctor can help you understand what to expect from treatment with LYNPARZA and bevacizumab.
  • Stay Organized – Using a planner can help you stay on track with taking your medication and going to your infusion appointments.
  • Write Notes – Taking regular notes of how you’re feeling can help you share any symptoms you may be experiencing with your doctor.
  • Keep Up the Conversation – Let your doctor know if you’re planning a trip or going through any other important changes in your life.

WHAT IS LYNPARZA?
LYNPARZA is a prescription medicine used to treat adults who have:
  • advanced ovarian cancer, fallopian tube cancer, or primary peritoneal cancer with a certain type of abnormal BRCA gene or a positive laboratory tumor test for genomic instability called HRD. LYNPARZA is used in combination with another anti-cancer medicine, bevacizumab, as maintenance treatment after the cancer has responded to your first treatment with platinum-based chemotherapy. Your healthcare provider will perform a test to make sure that LYNPARZA is right for you.
